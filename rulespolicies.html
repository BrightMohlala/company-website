Rules and Policies

This policy applies to the Witness & Sons Refrigeration & Air-conditioning and the company ensured that it enforces safe, responsible, and professional workplace. These policies also help in guaranteeing our workers and clients optimum interaction, security, and a sound workplace environment.

Workplace Policy

Our policy statement will involve creating awareness on how workers and the organization at large can have a healthier culture in the place of work. In the course of our operations, we embrace the business principles of teamwork, accountability, and business like attitude. At Witness & Sons, it will be everyone’s responsibility in the organisation to ensure they are aware of the culture of respect and cooperation in workplace.

  • Proffesionalism: It is mandatory that all our employees conduct themselves professionally and with courtesy to clients at all times.
  • Attendance & Punctuality: Employees must follow the working hours and attend all the appointments, meetings, and service visits on time. Maintaining punctuality is very important as we aim to give our clients a consistent service.
  • Dress Code: Workers are required to wear appropriate working clothes and protective accessories during surgeries. Apart from being used to boost professionalism among those in uniform, they are protective gear for workers in the field.

Expected Behaviour of Workers

At Witness & Sons, expectations of all employees are high, and we make sure that the best standards of behaviour are maintained. Every employee is expected to:

  • Respect Clients and Colleagues:
    • Employees are prohibited from using abusive language or rude behaviors towards clients, other employees or company management. Any form of prejudice and disrespectful behavour, such as harassment or discrimination will not be allowed.
  • Maintain Clean and Safe Work Areas: It is important that the areas where people work remain safe, and free of danger and every workspace must be left clean. Clutter free environment is important for safety and work ethic.
  • Adhere to Company Policies: Employees are required to adhere to various company regulations and polices, such as safety measures, work procedures, and customers’ relation policies.
  • Report Incidents: Any situation that occurs at the workplace, be it an accident or an unhealthy environment, should be escalated as soon as possible to avoid further occurrence of similar incidents.

HVAC Health and Safety Policy

The protection of our staff, customers, and all the community members remains our priority. Witness & Sons Refrigeration & Air-conditioning is an HVAC company whose primary responsibility is to ensure that all systems in the company are installed, maintained and repaired safely besides creating a safe working environment.

  • Safety Training: All employees work according to the current safety protocols in order to ensure they are following all industry rules and recommendations. This also refers to conduct concerning tools, equipment, and refrigerants as well as their proper operation.
  • Personal Protective Equipment (PPE): Employers must make sure that employees use appropriate PPE while being in contact with HVAC systems. For example, it entails the use of gloves, safety glasses, helmets, or other equipment needed to minimize risks that might be avoided by safety gears.
  • Risk Assessments: As a precaution measure, the method involves the assessment of possible risks that may happen during the course of the job and the necessary measures are taken to prevent the occurrence of risk factors. This does not only apply to sectors involving construction of houses but also sectors that involve construction of business entities.
  • Safe Handling of Refrigerants: We have guidelines on management of refrigerant and chemicals used in refrigeration and air conditioning and their proper disposal. Our team is also very knowledgeable about the rules governing the environmental status of a region and standard guidelines.
  • Emergency Procedures: During accidents or emergencies, employees are expected to follow the set protocols to eliminate delay when responding to the circumstances. First aid kits, and emergency contact numbers are easily accessible with every working site.
  • Client Safety: It is important to us to make sure that all HVAC systems are installed and operated in a safe manner. This includes checking for any leaks and proper insulation, checking for loose connections to reduce the risks for both our workers and clients.

At Witness & Sons, we fully understand that we have the responsibility of safety and professionalism.
Our policies are a clear demonstration of our standards to produce quality services to our clients,
and at the same time ensuring working conditions which uphold dignity for all employees.
