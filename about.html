About Witness & Sons

The company, Witness & Sons Refrigeration & Air-conditioning was established because of the desire to provide the best in cooling services.

Originally the company was only a small family business that over the years makes its way and becomes one of the most popular and reputable company that dealing with refrigeration and many other air conditioning service. Our founder, Witness, had a dream of a company that specializes in meeting the customers’ needs and delivering excellent work while also offering energy-saving products.

Out of the passion to deliver best service, we have grown in knowledge and area of specialization. Starting as a simple company that focuses on air conditioning services for residential areas, we have expanded our services to commercial and even industrial sections and providing comprehensive cooling solutions for establishments and homes as well as industries. It has remained the key to our success and impact that we are reckoned as the most reliable and excellent company for such services.

Operating Hours

Auto repair does not occur at a fixed time, which is why Witness & Sons provides services around the clock, so whenever you face a cooling system problem, you can be sure that you will receive professional help immediately. Whether you need an emergency with your auto or a simple check, we are prepared to help you.

  • Monday to Sunday: 24/7 Availability
Our team is always on call 24/7 to ensure that it can attend to all the necessary needs at all times, making us your one-stop supplier for all refrigeration and air-conditioning needs.

Non-Operational Days

Although we operate 24/7, our office and standard service teams will be unavailable on the following public holidays. However, emergency services will remain operational during these dates:

  • New Year’s Day (1 January)
  • Good Friday
  • Easter Monday
  • Freedom Day (27 April)
  • Worker’s Day (1 May)
  • Youth Day (16 June)
  • Heritage Day (24 September)
  • Christmas Day (25 December)
  • Day of Goodwill (26 December)
For any critical services, we keep our emergency response team available and functional during these days.
