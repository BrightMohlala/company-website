Services by Witness & Sons

Witness & Sons Refrigeration & Air-conditioning is a company that seeks to provide all the necessary services that you might require in a refrigerator and an air conditioning unit. For any kind of installation, maintenance, or repair that you need in your home or your workplace, we have a team of well-trained professionals who are willing to work for you.

1. Air-conditioning Services

Air-conditioning Installation

Looking to cool your home or your business? Our expert technicians install high-efficiency air conditioning systems for you. Some of the possibilities We provide are split systems, duct air conditioning, and central air systems. We will then evaluate your premises, determine the best system to install as well as provide you with excellent installations to enhance the performance of your HVAC.

Our air-conditioning installation services include:

  • Comprehensive site assessments
  • System recommendations tailored to your space
  • Professional installation of residential and commercial units
  • Energy-efficient air-conditioning solutions

Air-conditioning Repair & Maintenance

Many people depend on their AC unit to provide a cool and comfortable environment within the house as well as regulate living plus energy costs. We have standby repair crews for all your air-conditioning needs whether it is a simple repair, a small hiccup or a total system failure. It is also important to make sure that your system is always well maintained in an effort to increase its lifespan. Our main services include checkups, cleaning, and preventive measures as provided under our maintenance plans.

Our air-conditioning repair & maintenance services include:

  • Diagnosing and fixing system issues
  • Replacing faulty parts or components
  • Cleaning filters, coils, and ducts
  • Performance testing and system tune-ups

2. Refrigeration Services

Refrigeration Installation

Be it home refrigeration and air conditioning or complex commercial refrigeration systems, we provide professional installation services for proper functioning of cooling solutions. Whether you are undertaking a restaurant with a walk-in cooler or a grocery store with a refrigeration unit, it will be our pleasure to offer a professional solution install that fits your needs. Our refrigeration installation services include:

Refrigeration Repair & Maintenance
