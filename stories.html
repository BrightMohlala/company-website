Client Success Stories

1. Residential Air-conditioning Installation: The Smith Family

Johannesburg, Gauteng

The Smith family contacted Witness & Sons after they started experiencing multiple breakdowns with their old air conditioning system. They were in the need of better and efficient way to maintain comfortable temperature in their house during the hot summer days.

Solution:
In the case of both our independence and assessment our team visited the Smiths’ home and suggested that a high-efficiency split system unit would be ideal for their home. We managed to install the system within a day to reduce the distress on the family as much as possible. The result? Lessening their monthly electricity bill drastically and a comfortably cool home all year round.

Client Feedback:

Not a single objection can be raised to the service which was provided to us by the Witness & Sons! The new system installed is fantastic and the installation team was so courteous. Highly recommended! - Mrs. Smith

2. Commercial Refrigeration Overhaul: Bright Supermarket

Bushbuckridge, Mpumalanga

There had been problems experienced by the Bright Star Supermarket since the freezers within the commercial refrigeration systems had been becoming less efficient in the cooling process resulting to unsalable stock and higher energy demands. They needed a trustworthy partner to assist in re-establishing a different authoritative form of the system and to minimize their losses.

Solution:
As for the refrigeration system in the team of colleagues, it was changed to modern, energy-saving equipment. Also we introduced a proactive maintenance schedule to minimize occurrences of malfunctions and enhance operations of the systems.

Client Feedback:

Without a doubt Witness & Sons excelled all expectations! Long term we have been able to cut a lot of cost due to energy efficiency offered by our new refrigeration units and our stocks are always well conditioned. They have been very prompt on after-sales service! – Mr. Dube, Store Manager
